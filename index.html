About This Map

Air conditioning access is a critical factor in heat vulnerability. In NYC, more than 300 people die from heat-related causes each year — most are elderly, low-income, or rent their homes.

Use the layers above to explore how AC access correlates with income, poverty, renter status, race, and age across all 2,324 census tracts.

AC data: LACE 2023 (US Census Bureau & ASU). Socioeconomic data: ACS 5-Year 2022.
